The Collection

Each piece in this collection is individually thrown and sculpted, embracing the natural properties of porcelain and stoneware. The forms draw inspiration from classical pottery while incorporating contemporary elements and innovative glazing techniques.

Creation Process

Each vessel begins on the potter's wheel, where forms are thoughtfully crafted to balance function with artistic expression. After initial shaping, pieces undergo multiple firings and glazing processes, often incorporating traditional techniques with contemporary innovations.
