Lauren Silver

Ceramic Artist & Educator

Creating ceramic works that embody an understated eleganceâ€”intimate, personal pieces that give voice to unspoken observations and feelings. My goal is to convey deep sensibilities through careful touch and design.

Influenced by travels through Cyprus, Latin America, North Africa, and Europe, my work draws inspiration from natural forms: sea shells, marine life, fossils, and the intricate structures of spider webs, coral, and honeycombs. These elements reflect the ephemeral quality of human existence and the wonder of being alive.

Using stoneware, I create functional and decorative pieces that combine form, texture, and color. Each work is a quiet appreciation of nature, capturing organic shapes and the delicate balance between fragility and strength.
